Ugly Duckling

3.16.2020

Meshmixer & Sketchfab

For my 3D mashup, I was inspired by the story of the ugly duckling. I wanted to create a magical, but cute representation of the ending of the ugly duckling. In the children's book of the ugly duckling, the duckling was left out of every social group because other ducklings thought he was too ugly until one day, the ugly duckling grew up to become the most beautiful swan. This was what I wanted to represent with my 3D model.
